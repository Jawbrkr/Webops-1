                                      About

                                      WebOps Logistics is the industry's first medical device logistics platform that brings true mobility and connectivity to the entire enterprise, cycle count, and training tools. It's a secure, browser-based solution that requires no client-side software. What's more, it's extemely user-friendly for rapid user adoption. WebOps Logistics was built from the ground up by medical logistics experts for the mission-critical needs of the medical device industry. This real-time web-based solution manages your entire logistics cycle - from initial case planning to purchase order and replenishment.
